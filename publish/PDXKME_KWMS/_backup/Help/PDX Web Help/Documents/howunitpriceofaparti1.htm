How “Unit Price” of a part is calculated in case of Discount Rate settings?

 

If quotation is made with Discount Rates Settings, then unit price will be calculated  by following formulae:

 

Unit Price =   (Dnet Price) + (Premium Rate) – (Discount Rate of particular commodity group code)

 

In Case “Discount by Part Number” option is selected from Quotation Condition screen then “Discount Rate by Particular Commodity Code” will not be applied and discount mentioned against each line will be applied.
