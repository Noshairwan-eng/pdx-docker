Stock Transfer Request Details

 

Stock Transfer Request Details is the listing of parts along with part level information that are required in a stock transfer request. Prices are calculated from request detail screen. Accepted quantity and accepted part by KME, unit price of item, are calculate line wise. Then grand totals are defined on the bottom of the screen

After creating an inquiry successfully, its details are added.
