What is “Quotation Validity Date” and how it affects quotations?

Quotation validity date is the date up to which the subject quotation will remain valid. After this date, quotation will be expired and cannot be processed until validity date is revised by admin. Quotation validity defines that after the expiry of this quotation if prices change then KME will not be responsible. While creating a quotation, Quotation validity date is automatically set 7 days ahead.
