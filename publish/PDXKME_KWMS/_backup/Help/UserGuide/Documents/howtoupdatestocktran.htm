How to Update Stock Transfer Request Status?

 

Search an inquiry from Stock Transfer Request Inquiry screen and click on a record to view its options.

Press the Condition button; Stock Transfer Request Condition screen show record of selected inquiry. Select the Status as required and press Save button.

 

 

In-Process Requests:

For all such stock transfer requests, you can change the status to Confirmed after allocating parts from Details screen.

These requests can also be changed to Cancelled status. To cancel a request, first you need to delete all parts that are added in its detail.

 

Confirm Requests:

These stock transfer requests can be changed to In Process or Cancelled status.

To cancel a request, first you need to delete all parts that are added in its detail.

 

Convert To Order Requests:

The status of these requests cannot be changed.

 

Cancelled Requests:

The status of Cancelled requests cannot be changed.

 

Update the status as required and press Save button to apply the changes.
