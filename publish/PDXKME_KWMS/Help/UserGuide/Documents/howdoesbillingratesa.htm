How does “Billing Rates” affect pricing on quotation?

All parts are classified into different commodity groups and possibly different discount rates can be offered against each commodity group. Billing rates are the discount rates offered per distributor per customer and per order type. These discount rates are applied on list price. List price is multiplied by these billing rates and then unit price is calculated.

 

When Billing Rates option is selected on System Information, then this section is labeled as “Billing Rates by Commodity Group Codes”.

(Note: If a part doesn’t fall in any of this commodity group, then commodity group “A” billing rate will be applied).

 

 

In case Discount Rates option is selected on System Information, then this section is labeled as “Discount Rates by Commodity Group Codes”.

In this section, the discount percentage is displayed.

(Note: If a part doesn’t fall in any of this commodity group, then commodity group “Others” discount rate will be applied)
