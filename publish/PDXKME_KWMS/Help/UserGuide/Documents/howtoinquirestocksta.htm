How to Inquire Stock Status of Multiple Parts?

 

The Multiple Parts Stock Inquiry screen helps you to view stock status of multiple parts.

Open Master menu and press Multiple Parts Stock Inquiry to access the screen.

 

 

 

Enter all Part No that you want to search. You can provide up to 12 parts at a time. Now press the Search button; gird show available quantity in stock of selected parts along with their information.

 

 

 

If interchangeability exists with a part record, you can view all chained parts by selecting the record. The Interchangeability button becomes active for all such records.

 

 

 

Select a record for which interchangeability exists and press Interchangeability button; Stock Inquiry screen will show details of all chained parts.
