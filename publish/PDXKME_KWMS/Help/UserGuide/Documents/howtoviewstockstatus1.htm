How to View Stock Status of Multiple Parts in Warehouse?

 

The Warehouse Stock Status screen manages stock details of a part in warehouse.

Access the Warehouse Stock Status screen from Strategic Stock Menu.

Enter Part No and press Search button; stock status of specific part is listed.

 

 

Enter Part Numbers in fields. You can add up-to 12 parts at a time. Now press the Search button; grid will show the stock status of all provided parts.

 

 

If a part is a chained part, you can select it to view all parts in chain.
