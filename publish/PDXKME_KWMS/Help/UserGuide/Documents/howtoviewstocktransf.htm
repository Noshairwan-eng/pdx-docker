How to View Stock Transfer Inquiries?

 

The Stock Transfer Request Inquiry screen manages list of all stock inquiries that have been created. You can access the quotations from here and can update them.

Open Quotation menu and select Inquiry option to access Quotation Inquiry screen.

 

 

Click on the stock transfer record and you will view options for the selected record.
