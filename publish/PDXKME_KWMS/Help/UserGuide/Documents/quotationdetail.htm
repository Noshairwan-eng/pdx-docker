Quotation Detail

 

Quotation detail contains list/details of requested items with part level information. It is to inform KME about parts and their quantity that are required in a quotation condition. Prices are calculated from quotation detail screen. Accepted quantity and accepted part by KME, unit price of item, discounts and taxes etc. are calculated line wise. Then the grand totals are defined on the bottom of the screen.

 

Access the screen from Quotation menu.

 

 

 

 

You can also access the screen from Detail button on Quotation Inquiry screen.
