How to Update Consignment Order Status?

 

Search an inquiry from Consignment Order Inquiry screen and click on a record to view its options.

Press the Condition button; Consignment Condition screen show record of selected inquiry. Select the Status as required and press Save button.

 

 

 

In-Process Orders:

For all such consignment orders, you can change the status to Confirmed after allocating parts from Details screen.

These orders can also be changed to Cancelled status. To cancel an order, first you need to delete all parts that are added in its detail.

 

 

Confirm Orders:

These consignment orders can be changed to In Process or Cancelled status.

To cancel an order, first you need to delete all parts that are added in its detail.

 

Cancelled Orders:

The status of Cancelled orders cannot be changed.

 

Invoiced Orders:

The status of these requests cannot be changed.

 

 

Update the status as required and press Save button to apply the changes.
