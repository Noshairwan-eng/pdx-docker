How to Copy a Quotation to Sales Order?

 

All quotation inquiries that are confirmed will be converted to Sales Order for further processing.

Click on Copy to So button for a Confirmed quotation, following process will be performed at backend.

1.  Allocate the stock from warehouse and available on order

2.  Create picking instruction for allocated parts

3.  And, perform packing process for picked parts

 

 

The status of respective record will be changed to Copy to Sales Order.
