Quotation

 

Sales process starts from quotation. Quotation is used to facilitate the Customer (Distributor) to get pricing information of one or more parts. This module is further divided into Quotation Inquiry, Quotation Condition and Quotation Detail.

 

Quotation can be changed/updated before its confirmation. It remains valid for one week after creation, and then gets expired.
