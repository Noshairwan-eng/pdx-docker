How does “Billing Rates” affect pricing on quotation?

All parts are classified into different commodity groups and possibly different discount rates can be offered against each commodity group. Billing rates are the discount rates offered per distributor per order type. These discount rates are applied on list price. List price is multiplied by these billing rates and then unit price is calculated.

 

(Note: If a part doesn’t fall in any of this commodity group then commodity group “A” billing rate will be applied)
