What are the options for downloading the invoice?

From Sales Invoice Inquiry screen, provide criteria and search records.

The searched sales invoice records can be downloaded in CSV and XLS format.

 

 

Download CSV:

Open Download options and press CSV. A success message is displayed, and available records are exported in CSV format.

 

 

 

 

Download XLS:

Open Download options and press XLS. A success message is displayed, and available records are exported in XLS format.
