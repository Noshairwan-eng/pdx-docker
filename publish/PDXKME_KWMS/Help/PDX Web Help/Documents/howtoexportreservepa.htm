How to export reserve parts information?

From Reserve Parts screen, provide criteria and search records.

The searched reserve part records can be downloaded in CSV and XLS format.

 

 

Download CSV:

Open Download options and select CSV. A success message is displayed, and available records are exported in CSV format.

 

 

 

 

Download XLS:

Open Download options and select XLS. A success message is displayed, and available records are exported in XLS format.
